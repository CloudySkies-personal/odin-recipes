Leche Flan

Description

Leche Flan is a Filipino reimagination of the Spanish cr√®me caramel. It is a simple dish that can be prepared with simple ingredients, earning it a spot as a staple dish for restaurant menus and special occassions.

Ingredients

  • 10 pieces of eggs
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 cup of fresh milk
  • 1 cup of granulate sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Separate the yolk from the egg whites of all the eggs
  2. Collect all the yolk into a bowl and beat them with a fork for egg beater
  3. Add the condensed milk to the yolk mixture and stir thoroughly
  4. Add the milk and vanilla to the mixture then mix well
  5. Heat a Leche Flan mold on low fire, then mix some sugar onto the mold until they caramellize
  6. Spread the caramel evenly on the mold
  7. Allow 5 minutes for the caramel to cool before pouring the egg mixture into the mold
  8. Cover the top of the mold using an Aluminum foil
  9. Steam the mold for 30 to 35 minutes
  10. After steaming, let the mold cool down, then place it into the refrigerator
