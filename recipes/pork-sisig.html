Pork Sisig

Description

Pork sisig is a Filipino dish that can be served as either a main dish or an appetizer lnown for its salty and meaty taste. It is composed mainly of minced pork meat, then topped with onions, chilli, and sometimes egg.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pig ears
  • 1 lb pig snout
  • 1 lb pork belly
  • 2 pieces of minced onions
  • 3 pieces bay leaves
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 4 thumbs of crushed ginger
  • 1 quarts water
