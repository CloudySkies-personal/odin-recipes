Pork Sisig

Description

Pork sisig is a Filipino dish that can be served as either a main dish or an appetizer lnown for its salty and meaty taste. It is composed mainly of minced pork meat, then topped with onions, chilli, and sometimes egg.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pig ears
  • 1 lb pig face
  • 1 lb pork belly
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 3 pieces bay leaves
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/4 cup kalamansi
  • 1/2 tablespoon pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Combine pork belly, face, ears, vinegar, soy sauce in a stock pot. Add enough water to cover the meat, then add bay leaves, salt, and peppercorns. Bring to a boil then simmer for atleast an hour or until meat is soft.
  2. Remove meat from pot and grill for 10 minutes.
  3. Chop the pork belly and face meat into 1/2 sized cubes, and finely chop the pig ears.
  4. Mix the chopped meat in a bowl, then add chilis, kalamansi juice, vinegar, salt, pepper, and garlic powder
  5. Add chopped pork (with liquids) to saute pan, saute until meat is hot. Add mayonnaise and combine.
